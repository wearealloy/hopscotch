About

Hopscotch San Antonio is a permanent 20,000 sq foot gallery which features immersive and interactive art experiences. We create platforms for local, national and international artists to experiment with a wide spectrum of mediums and technology. Our aim is to elicit a sense of joy and wonder in the spaces we curate; where our guests may explore beyond their day to day reality.
