Eat

Smack’s Chicken Shack is a concept built around large fried chicken sandwiches! The set menu consists of items such as Nashville Hot, Big Smack, and the very popular loaded fries. Smack’s Chicken Shack runs new specials weekly and will be featuring a specialty “Let’s Hopscotch” sandwich. They are known for their creativity and were named “Best of San Antonio Food Truck” in 2019 and runner-up in 2020.

Drink

Our beverage features specialty cocktails, beer, wine and non-alcoholic beverages that were carefully selected and inspired by our installations throughout the space.
Ticket holders may take beverages with them throughout the gallery space or enjoy them in the lounge on the main floor. The lounge is also open to the public so that non-ticket holders can enjoy it. The Lounge is 18+ after 7pm.

Shop

At our onsite gift shop gallery guests, locals and tourists alike can find unique gifts and custom curiosities. Along with selling Hopscotch merchandise, we have partnered with local artists to offer unique items. Many of the installation artists will also sell their other works in the gift shop.
